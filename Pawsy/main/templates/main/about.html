About Pawsy Animal Shelter

We are dedicated to finding loving homes for animals in need.

Our Mission

At Pawsy Animal Shelter, our mission is to provide care and shelter for abandoned, abused, or surrendered animals, and to find them permanent, loving homes. We are committed to promoting responsible pet ownership through education and community outreach.

Our History

Founded in 2025, Pawsy Animal Shelter began as a small rescue operation run by a group of passionate animal lovers. Over the years, we have grown into a full-service shelter, helping hundreds of animals find their forever homes each year.
